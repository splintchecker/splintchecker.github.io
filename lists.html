Splint Mailing Lists

There are two mailing lists associated with Splint.

Splint Announce

This list is very low volume (averages about 1 message per year), and reserved for announcements of new versions of Splint. Everyone using Splint should be on this list.

To subscribe to the the list, fill out the following form. You will be sent email requesting confirmation, to prevent others from gratuitously subscribing you.
