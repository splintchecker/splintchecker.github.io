There is a syntax for LCL in
   ftp://ftp.sds.lcs.mit.edu/pub/lclint/lcl-syntax.ps[.gz].

The definitive reference on LCL is:

   Tan, Yang Meng.  Formal Specification Techniques for
   Engineering Modular C, Kluwer International Series in Software
   Engineering, Volume 1, Kluwer Academic Publishers, Boston, 1995.

There is also a chapter on LCL as well as an overview of the Larch
family of specification languages in:

   Guttag, John V. and Horning, James J., with Stephen J. Garland, 
   Kevin D. Jones, Andreas Modet, and Jeannette M. Wing, Larch:
   Languages and Tools for Formal Specification, Springer-Verlag, 
   Texts and Monographs in Computer Science, 1993.

(Sorry, neither of these is available electronically because of
publisher's copyrights.)
